The CyberScots are a robotics team from Piedmont, CA that participates in the FIRST Tech Challenge. We are building a robot to compete in the tournament later this year. Our team is separated into a hardware team and a software team. The hardware team builds the robot and the software team writes code to help it interact with its environment.
